Dylan Wallace Band

Dylan is a country singer, songwriter and guitarist hailing from Calgary, Alberta. He has been playing music since the age of ten when he first picked up a guitar and started singing and writing his own songs shortly after. In 2018, Dylan decided to pursue his true passion for music. He has spent the past 5 years releasing multiple singles, an EP and playing some renowned events with his band (The Horseshoe Tavern, Magna Hoedown, River Run Theatre). He has developed his classic rock inspired country sound which ties together intricate guitar work with effortless, powerful vocals. Dylan is no stranger to Alapalooza, performing on our lakeside stage during the 2020 and 2021 covid years, and with his father Steve in Gap33 before that. Dylan moved to Calgary last year but is flying back to play at Alapalooza 2023. Joining him on keyboards and vocals is Porcupine Bay cottager (and father) Steve Wallace (returningfor his 7 th show at Alapalooza), Matt McWaters (bass), Matt Del Grosso (drums) and Dave Mordak (guitar).

Fiddlestix

Steve Bowen, Yamaha endorsed electric fiddler, and his award winning team blend the energy of a Rock Concert with Celtic, Country and Folk music creating what has been dubbed 'FIDDLE-ROCK' by Y108 Rocks Hamilton. With Bowen's unparalleled violin skills, Steve and the band are defining Fiddlestix as one of the top Canadian bands due to their signature sound, instrumental layering, multiple lead vocal trade-offs, and incredible four-part harmonies, flexing genres and blending styles seamlessly.   Fiddlestix recently won the Milton Reader’s Choice 2023 Platinum Award for best local band and have won numerous local awards since 2019. Fiddlestix has shared the stage with some of Canada's finest musicians, including Big Sugar, Leahy, Gord Downie, Great Big Sea, Jim Cuddy, The Irish Rovers, Honeymoon Suite and many more. Fiddlestix headlined the CNE East Coast Kitchen Party 2018 and 2019 and performed an astonishing run of 36 shows over 5 days for the Celtic Festival at the CNE in 2022. Joining Steve Bowen (electric violin, vocals) are Nancy Cipollone (guitar, vocals), Mike Altmann (bass, vocals), Scott Apted (drums, vocals), and Jeff Blake (keys, vocals). If you missed Fiddlestix at my 60th Birthday bash last fall, here’s your chance to see them live at Alapalooza! You don’t want to miss this high energy show!

The Duomatics

The Duomatics are a Montreal area acoustic duo, specializing in great rock and pop songs from the 60's to today.  Multi-instrumentalist and vocalists, Peter Bowering (vocals, guitar, drums) and John Gurnsey (drums, vocals, guitar, piano) literally switch it up during the show, switching instruments for more variety and entertainment value. The Duomatics were a big hit at Alapalooza 2022. We are excited to have them back again this year! They will also be playing on Monday August 7 th on White Trout Bay. Be sure to boat up and watch them there as well.

Kristen Prince

Kristen is a Canadian saxophone player and singer. She has performed with many artists including the Arkells, Canadian legend Andy Kim (Sugar Sugar), Lowest of the Low, Suzie McNeil, My Son the Hurricane, and 4-time Juno nominee Tom Barlow. Kristen plays regularly in the Port Credit area with The Dedbeats as well as duo and solo gigs. She is currently on a 37 day North American tour with My Son the Hurricane. Kristen has performed 4 times at Alapalooza with The Tom Barlow Band. Last year she stole the show with her incredible solo performance on the water. For 2023 she is back as a solo artist. Can’t wait to see what’s new this year!
